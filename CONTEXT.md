# LaunchBlitz

LaunchBlitz takes a founder from a raw business idea to a launch-ready handoff in one guided session. This document is the shared language for the product's public and in-product surfaces.

## Language

**Launch packet**:
The umbrella deliverable a single session produces — the complete bundle of launch assets handed to the founder.
_Avoid_: Build packet, launch kit (as the umbrella name)

**Session**:
One guided run from idea capture through the staged workflow to the launch packet.
_Avoid_: Run, job

**Launch Kit**:
One named asset *inside* the launch packet (a launch-readiness summary). Not the packet itself.

**Lovable-ready**:
Qualifier describing the landing-page export specifically — shaped for handoff into Lovable's build mode. Applies to the landing-page asset, not the whole packet.

**Stage**:
One step of the workflow (Idea capture, Market validation, Customer avatar, Positioning, Copy deck, Brand inputs, Lovable export, Launch kit). Some stages are still scaffolded in the MVP.

## Relationships

- A **Session** produces exactly one **Launch packet**
- A **Launch packet** contains named assets: Market Validation, Customer Avatar, Copy Deck, a **Lovable-ready** Landing Page Export, and a **Launch Kit**
- A **Session** runs through ordered **Stages**

## Flagged ambiguities

- "Build packet" (hero card) vs "launch packet" (issue #6) vs "Launch Kit" (deliverable) were used interchangeably — resolved: **Launch packet** is the umbrella; **Launch Kit** is one asset inside it.
