# LaunchBlitz

LaunchBlitz takes a founder from a raw business idea to a launch-ready handoff in one guided session. This document is the shared language for the product's public and in-product surfaces.

## Language

**Launch packet**:
The umbrella deliverable a **Build** produces — the complete bundle of launch assets handed to the founder.
_Avoid_: Build packet, launch kit (as the umbrella name)

**Build**:
A persisted founder project — one idea worked from capture through to a launch packet. This is the unit counted by plan (Free = 1 build, Builder = 10 builds), listed at `/builds`, and resumable across sittings.
_Avoid_: Project, workspace (as the countable unit)

**Session**:
One guided working run through the stages of a **Build**. A build can span multiple sessions (a founder can resume it), but the MVP promise is that a build is finishable in a single session.
_Avoid_: Run, job

**Launch Kit**:
One named asset *inside* the launch packet (a launch-readiness summary). Not the packet itself.

**Lovable-ready**:
Shaped for handoff into Lovable's build mode. "**Lovable-ready launch packet**" is the product's headline phrase (per issue #6): the packet is marketed as Lovable-ready because its landing-page export is the direct Lovable handoff artifact. When precision matters (e.g. describing which asset actually opens in Lovable), scope the term to that landing-page export.

**Stage**:
One step of the workflow (Idea capture, Market validation, Customer avatar, Positioning, Copy deck, Brand inputs, Lovable export, Launch kit). Some stages are still scaffolded in the MVP.

## Relationships

- A **Build** is worked through one or more **Sessions** and yields exactly one **Launch packet**
- A **Launch packet** contains named assets: Market Validation, Customer Avatar, Copy Deck, a **Lovable-ready** Landing Page Export, and a **Launch Kit**
- A **Session** runs through ordered **Stages**

## Flagged ambiguities

- "Build packet" (hero card) vs "launch packet" (issue #6) vs "Launch Kit" (deliverable) were used interchangeably — resolved: **Launch packet** is the umbrella; **Launch Kit** is one asset inside it.
- Does "Lovable-ready" qualify the whole **Launch packet** or only the landing-page export? — resolved: it's marketing shorthand for the packet (the phrase "Lovable-ready launch packet" is intentional per issue #6); the literal handoff artifact is the landing-page export.
- "Build" vs "Session" — resolved: a **Build** is the persisted, plan-counted project unit (pricing "10 builds", the `/builds` list); a **Session** is one working run through it. Pricing correctly counts **Builds**, not sessions.
